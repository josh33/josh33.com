Privacy

Mongolian Vocab does not require an account and does not use third-party analytics. Your dictionary and learning progress are stored locally on your device.

FAQs

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Mongolian Vocab is designed to work without an internet connection. Your dictionary and learning progress are stored locally on your device.

Will an update overwrite my personal dictionary?

No. Your custom words, edits, and learning progress are stored separately from the built-in dictionary. Updates should not overwrite your personal changes.

How do dictionary packs work?

Dictionary packs let you add optional word bundles (like Animals). You can preview a pack and choose whether to add it. Packs are meant to augment your dictionary without removing your existing edits.

How do I report a translation issue?

Email support with the English word, the Mongolian text, and what you believe the correct translation should be. If possible, include a screenshot.

Can I request new word categories?

Yes—send a note with the category name (e.g., “food”, “travel”, “family”) and 10-20 suggested words.

I found a bug—what should I include?

Please include: what you were doing, what you expected, what happened instead, and your iOS version + device model.
